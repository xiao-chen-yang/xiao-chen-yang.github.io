Information for MSc (Research) and PhD applicants

  • The following two webpages describe the MSc (Research) and PhD programmes and application procedure at the School of Mathematics and Statistics, University of Glasgow.

    • https://www.gla.ac.uk/postgraduate/research/statistics/
    https://www.gla.ac.uk/schools/mathematicsstatistics/research/postgraduate/
     

  • The minimum entry requirement is a UK 2:1 degree (or equivalent) in a relevant subject area (including but not limited to Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, and Electrical Engineering). Applicants should have a strong academic background in Mathematics/Statistics or proficiency in programming, as well as good knowledge in machine learning.

  • Feel free to email me to discuss your application. In your email, please state your research interests and include your CV, transcripts for undergraduate and postgraduate studies, undergraduate and postgraduate dissertations, and publications (if any).

For PhD applicants:

  • Funding opportunities are available but competitive. A first-class degree is typically expected to be eligible for scholarship. Shortlisting and review of applications for scholarships will begin on 1 January 2024.
